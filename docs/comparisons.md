## Reinforcement Learning Alignment
Traditional alignment approaches rely on reward optimization. TC proposes that long-term stability may depend less on reward maximization and more on coherence preservation across interacting agents.

## Game Theory
Game-theoretic models analyze strategic interaction between agents. TC extends this perspective by emphasizing systemic coherence as a stabilizing attractor rather than purely competitive equilibrium.

## Systems Theory
TC aligns with systems thinking by viewing agents as embedded within larger constraint networks, where stability emerges from balanced interaction rather than isolated optimization.
